Microdermabrasion

For uneven skin tone, wrinkles, and rough skin texture

Best for exfoliation, dermabrasion is a cosmetic procedure aimed at improving skin texture and reducing fine lines by gently abrading the surface of the skin. Post-treatment, the skin displays a smoother, more even appearance, enhancing overall complexion and radiance.

Our professional team uses a handheld device that carefully polishes the skin, effectively enhancing its naturally smooth texture.
